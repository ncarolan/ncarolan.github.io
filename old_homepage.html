I'm an engineer and researcher working on machine intelligence and perception.

My interests lie in building intelligent systems that can learn rich representations from data and reason over those representations to solve fundamental problems. I'm currently working on projects in interpretability, representation learning, and computer vision.

Previously, I worked on image generation/understanding and photo editing at Google, where I contributed to research on "rich human feedback" for generative models that won a CVPR best paper award and multiple Google tech impact awards. Before that, I studied computer science at Amherst College.
